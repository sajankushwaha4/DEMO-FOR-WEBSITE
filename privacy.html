Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. We protect your data with enterprise-grade security.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Quick Navigation

1. Introduction

GLOBSI India Sourcing Private Limited ("GLOBSI", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website globsiindia.com, use our services, or otherwise interact with us.

Key Commitment: We never sell your personal data. Your information is used solely to provide excellent sourcing services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, location
  • Business Data: RFQs, technical specifications, sourcing requirements, drawings
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent (via cookies)
  • Communication Records: Emails, chat messages, call logs (for service improvement)
We do not collect sensitive personal data (race, religion, health) unless explicitly required for business compliance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to:

  • Process and respond to RFQs and business inquiries
  • Provide quotations, samples, and sourcing solutions
  • Communicate project updates and delivery status
  • Improve our sourcing platform and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Information Sharing

We share information only when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Verified manufacturers for RFQ fulfillment (under strict NDA)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Indian law or court order
  • Business Transfers: During company merger/acquisition
No Data Sales: We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties for marketing.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP compliant)

6. International Data Transfers

Data is processed primarily in India. International transfers use Standard Contractual Clauses and comply with applicable laws.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request data removal ("right to be forgotten")
  • Objection: Object to processing
  • Portability: Receive data in structured format

Exercise Rights: Email privacy@globsiindia.com. Response within 30 days.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality. Analytics cookies (optional) improve experience.

  • Manage via browser settings or cookie consent banner
  • No targeted advertising cookies

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are business-to-business. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

10. Data Retention

Data retained only as necessary:

  • RFQ records: 7 years (legal compliance)
  • Contact info: Until unsubscribe or deletion request
  • Usage data: 12 months

11. Policy Updates

Updated January 1, 2025. Continued use constitutes acceptance. Major changes notified via email.
